What is RGB?

RGB is back and better than ever, as... Reach, Grow, and Build: How to Break Into the Design Industry!


Join Innovative Design on March 12 for a day of learning from industry recruiters and designers, through a series of talks, workshops, and open Q&A. Professional designers who have worked at Google, Khan Academy, Palantir and more(!) will share their experiences in both entering and working in the industry. We will also feature a recruiter who will give insight on what companies are looking for. The speaker series will be followed by workshops taught by both speakers and other guests, and will focus on how to build yourself professionally.


The event will be at Berkeley's brand new Jacobs Hall For Design Innovation. Food & Drinks will be provided, fun will be had, and minds will be blown.


*Cal ID required for entry.
