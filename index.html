What is RGB?

Join Innovative Design on Sunday, March 12 for a day of learning from industry designers, through a series of talks, workshops, and open Q&A. Professional designers who have worked at Facebook, Ueno, Palantir and more(!) will share their experiences in both entering and working in the industry, as well as give insight on what companies are looking for. The speaker series will be followed by workshops taught by speakers and other designers, and will focus on how to build yourself professionally.


The event will be at Berkeley's Jacobs Hall For Design Innovation. Food & drinks will be provided, fun will be had, and minds will be blown.
